Best Roland-Garros of all time?

Sunday, June 8th, 2025 Carlos Alcaraz claimed his second Roland Garros victory in a five set thriller against Jannik Sinner. Was this the best match we have scene since the Federer vs Nadal battle at Wimbledon 2008?

Facts about the Match

  1. Length
    • The championship match lasted 5 hours and 29 minutes
    • It broke the record for longest French Open final in history
  2. Competitors
    • Carlos Alcaraz
    • Jannik Sinner
  3. Spectators
    • Over seven million people tuned in to watch the championship match on television
    • 15,000 People packed the Stade Roland Garros, filling every seat in the stadium.

Carlos Alcaraz

  • 22 years old
  • Sponsored by Nike and Babolat
  • Won $2.9 million in Prize Money after winning the French Open
  • First time in his career to come back after losing the first two sets
